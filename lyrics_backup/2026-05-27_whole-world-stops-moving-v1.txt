[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
It’s just a shadow in the fading light
A sudden wonder in the cozy night
You lay your fingers in the palm of mine
And the whole world stops moving in time

[Verse 1]
The fire is fading in the open room
But there’s a quiet heat we both consume
You trace a circle on my coffee cup
And when you smile, the quiet opens up

[Pre-Chorus]
No need to rush where we are going now
We have the midnight on our side somehow
Just pull a little closer to my chest
And let the heavy world just take a rest

[Chorus]
Oh, the whole world stops moving when you’re here
Everything around us starts to disappear
It’s just the gravity of you and me
Caught inside a timeless memory

[Post-Chorus]
Stops moving when I pull you near
[Echo] (Starts to disappear)

[Verse 2]
You lean in closer like a gentle breeze
And bring my racing spirit to its knees
I feel the warmth upon your fingertips
Before the silence settles on your lips

[Pre-Chorus 2]
No need to rush where we are going now
We have the midnight on our side somehow
Just pull a little closer to my chest
And let the heavy world just take a rest

[Chorus]
Oh, the whole world stops moving when you’re here
Everything around us starts to disappear
It’s just the gravity of you and me
Caught inside a timeless memory

[Post-Chorus 2]
Stops moving when I pull you near
[Echo] (Starts to disappear)

[Bridge]
I wandered far to find a gentle spark
Looking for a beacon in the quiet dark
But all of my wandering was destined to lead
Right here to you, from yours truly, Saeed
So let the morning take its sweet time waking
There is beauty in the memories we are making

[Spoken]
[Spoken] (I don't want to be anywhere else)
[Whispered] (Just right here with you)

[Final Chorus]
Oh, the whole world stops moving when you’re here
Everything around us starts to disappear
It’s just the gravity of you and me
Caught inside a timeless memory
